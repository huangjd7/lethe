13 * particles. This is used to identify which index of the ParticleHandler
14 * corresponds to which properties. Two types of solvers are currently
15 * supported. DEM implies pure DEM simulation whereas cfd_dem is used to
16 * indicate simulations in which the particles are coupled to CFD (CFD-DEM).
17 * In the latter case the particles carry additional properties related to the
18 * particle-fluid coupling.
